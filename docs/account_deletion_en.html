PrayStory Account Deletion

Effective: July 16, 2026

Users of PrayStory (developer contact: shjoung0@gmail.com) can delete their account directly from within the app.

1. How to delete your account

  1. Open the PrayStory app
  2. Go to the [Settings] tab
  3. Select [Account] > [Delete Account]
  4. Confirm deletion in the popup

2. Data that gets deleted

Once account deletion is complete, the following data is permanently and immediately deleted with no retention period.

Data typeDetails
Account credentialsEmail address, password, linked social login (Google/Kakao) information
Profile informationName, birth year, gender, church
Service usage recordsAll prayer journal entries
Community dataAuthored community letters/notices, groups you own (deleted entirely), your membership in other groups
Support recordsSubmitted feedback history

PrayStory has no payment or e-commerce functionality, so there are no transaction records that must be retained under applicable law. All data listed above is deleted immediately upon request.

3. Deleting individual data without deleting your account

You can delete the following data directly in the app without deleting your account.

Data typeHow to delete
An individual prayer entryOpen the entry from the [Letter] or [Prayer Log] tab > tap [Delete]
A community letter you wroteGroup detail > [Letters] tab > the × icon on your own letter
A group notice you wroteIf you are the group owner: Group detail > [Notices] tab > the × icon
Leave a specific group onlyGroup detail > menu > [Leave group] (your other groups and account data are unaffected)
Profile info (church, gender, birth year)[Settings] > [Profile] — edit or clear the field

Individually deleted data is removed immediately and is not retained.

4. Contact

For questions about account deletion, contact shjoung0@gmail.com.
