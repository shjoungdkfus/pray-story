```
📖 PrayStory — Your Daily Letters of Prayer

PrayStory turns your daily prayers into something like a handwritten letter.
Inspired by the feeling of a leather-bound Bible being written, day by day,
through you — every screen is designed to feel warm, quiet, and reverent.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✍️ A daily prayer letter
· A two-page, Bible-like home screen
· Swipe left and right to move through your prayers by date
· A ruled writing screen with the feel of a handwritten letter
· Adjustable font size for comfortable reading

🙏 The joy of answered prayer
· Check off answered prayers and remember every faithful answer
· Stats like "N days recorded this month" and "N-day streak" to keep you going
· Monthly and weekly calendars show every day you prayed at a glance

⏰ Prayer reminders
· Daily prayer alarms at times you choose
· Set multiple times, toggle each on or off individually

💌 A community that prays together
· Create a prayer group or join one with an invite code
· Share letters in a public feed or within your group
· Send an anonymous "For Someone Dear" letter to encourage someone

🌙 A screen that fits you
· Light and dark themes
· A warm cream light mode, and a deep midnight dark mode for late-night prayer

🔐 Getting started is easy
· Sign in with Kakao or Google in seconds
· Email sign-up also supported

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

With PrayStory, the prayers you might otherwise let slip away
become your own letters, gathered one day at a time.
Start writing today's prayer.
```
