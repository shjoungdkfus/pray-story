PrayStory Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

PrayStory (the "App") values your privacy and complies with applicable data protection laws, including South Korea's Personal Information Protection Act. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used and stored.

1. Information We Collect

CategoryItemsCollected at
Account informationEmail address, social login identifier (Kakao/Google), nickname and profile image (if provided by Kakao)Sign-up / social login
Profile informationName, birth year, gender, church affiliation (optional)Onboarding / profile edit
Service usage informationPrayer entries you write, prayer answer status, community letters and group informationWhile using the service
Inquiry informationFeedback content and categoryWhen sending feedback
  • The App does not collect sensitive device data such as location, contacts, or photo library.
  • When you sign in with Kakao, your email is used solely to identify your account.

2. Purpose of Use

  • Member identification and login session management
  • Providing core services: prayer records, statistics, and reminders
  • Providing community features (groups, letters)
  • Responding to inquiries (feedback) and improving the service

3. Retention and Deletion

  • Your data is retained until you delete your account.
  • Upon account deletion (withdrawal), related personal information is deleted without delay, except where retention is required by applicable law, in which case it is kept for the required period.
  • You can delete your account directly in the App via Settings > Account > Delete Account.

4. Sharing with Third Parties / Data Processors

The App does not sell or arbitrarily share your personal information with third parties. The following services are used to provide data storage and authentication:

ProcessorFunctionLocation
Supabase Inc.Database storage, user authenticationCloud
Kakao / GoogleSocial login authenticationCloud

5. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • View and edit your personal information (via in-app profile edit)
  • Delete your prayer records and other data
  • Delete your personal information by withdrawing your account

6. Security Measures

  • All communication is encrypted via HTTPS.
  • The database is protected with Row Level Security (RLS) policies so that each user can only access their own data.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be revised to reflect changes in law or the service. Any changes will be announced in the App or on this page.
